О компании

SLAV AG is an Austrian based holding company with operations in over 20 countries around the world. Listed on the Vienna Stock Exchange, SLAV AG has a proud history of successful businesses across industry, finance, real estate and renewable energy that ensure readiness to meet the challenges of today and deliver solutions for tomorrow.

Originally founded in 1992 in the Ukrainian industrial sector with the guiding principles of reliability, transparency and efficiency, SLAV AG has grown to be one of the largest and most diversified investment and financial holdings in Ukraine.

With assets primarily in Austria and Ukraine, SLAV AG group companies are able to service a wide range of international clients and markets, always striving for excellence and innovation.

The core strategy of the Group is to develop and manage industry leaders and leverage their broad collective expertise and knowledge for dynamic, prosperous and long-term growth.
